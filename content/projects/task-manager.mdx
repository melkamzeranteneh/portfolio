## Goal

To build a simplified yet functional alternative to Trello for small teams, focusing on the user experience of drag-and-drop interactions.

### Stack

- **Drag and Drop**: `@dnd-kit/core` for accessibility and performance.
- **State Management**: Zustand for simple, global state.
- **Auth & DB**: Firebase for quick backend setup.

### Result

The final application supports multiple boards, lists, and cards, with instantaneous updates across devices.
